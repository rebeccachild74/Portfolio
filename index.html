About Me

I'm an aspiring Software Developer with a passion for problem solving and empowering women in STEM. I am set to graduate the Washington State University's Everett campus in December of 2020 with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Software Engineering and a minor in Mathematics. Outside of school, I work as a Computer Science tutor at Everett Community College, serve as Secretary to both SWE and the ACM, and work on artwork. My professional goals include obtaining an internship and finding a full time position where my work improves the quality of people's lives.
